You are given a random target number at the start of the game.
There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a spacific amount of points to your total score.
You win the game by matching your total score to ransom number , you lose the game of your total score goes above the random number.
The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you clock on it.
Each time when the game starts, the game will changee the value of esch crystal.
You can click the "I Give Up" button at any time to forfeit the round.
